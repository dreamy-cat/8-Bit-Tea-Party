# Welcome to 8-Bit Tea Party!

 Welcome to 8-Bit Tea Party! We create retro games and demos on good old platforms like ZX Spectrum, IBM-PC, etc. Review a classic games and new releases by community on retro scene. And sometimes just a little bit of chat with hot tea! All materials under Creative Commons: attribution, non commercial, share alike (CC BY-NC-SA) type of license. Feel free to use, update and share the culture of 8-Bit Tea Party! :)
